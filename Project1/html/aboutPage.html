CriC is a crisis situation management tool, designed to help authorities to easily inform citizens about incoming natural disasters and other unavoidable dangers, as well as make information about missing persons early accessible in order to quicken the search operations.
CriC uses the Google Crisis Map service to provide up to date information about current or incoming threats. We also use the Google Person Finder service to implement our missing persons feature.
CriC was made as a project for the Web Tehnologies laboratory. It was made by four students: Andrei Ghiran, Cristina Cernat, Ioana Balan and Leandro Vega Palma.
